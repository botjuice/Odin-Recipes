spicy japanese wasabi deviled eggs (16 servings)

my homeade deviled eggs

INGREDIENTS

  • eggs
  • mayo
  • green onions
  • rice wine vinegar
  • wasabi paste
  • salt
  • fresh pea shoots
  • pickled ginger slices

STEPS

  1. boil eggs then peel
  2. slice eggs in half, remove yolks and place in bowl
  3. mash yolks until smooth, mix with mayo, vinegar, and wasabi paste
  4. add salt
  5. spoon yolk mixture into egg whites, garnish with pickled ginger and pea shoots
