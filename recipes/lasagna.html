my homeade lasagna

INGREDIENTS

  • chicken breast
  • lime juicee
  • taco seasoning
  • shredded mozzerella
  • sour cream
  • salsa
  • salt and pepper
  • lasagna noodles

STEPS

  1. combine seasoning
  2. cook chicken for 15 minutes
  3. mix cheese, sour cream and salsa in a bowl
  4. put sour cream in baking dish, then add mozzerella
  5. bake in a preheated oven
